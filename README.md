# Know Before You Switch

**Compare your prescription costs between Horizon BCBSNJ and Redirect Health plans.**

A simple, mobile-friendly tool that lets you look up your medications and see a side-by-side cost comparison before your benefits change.

## How to Use

1. Visit the site
2. Search for each medication you or your family takes
3. Adjust the count if multiple family members take the same drug
4. See your projected monthly and annual cost difference
5. Copy and share your results

## Data Sources

- **Horizon BCBSNJ Classic Formulary** (October 2025) - Tier 1/2/3 copay structure: $15/$50/$75
- **Redirect Health Master Rx Formulary** (January 2023) - EverydayCARE with Hospital plan

## Important Notes

- Actual costs may vary by pharmacy, dosage, and quantity
- Medications not on the Redirect formulary require full cash payment and do **not** count toward the out-of-pocket maximum
- The Redirect formulary was last updated January 2023; newer generics may not appear
- This tool is for informational purposes only. Verify costs with your plan administrator.

## Deployment

This is a static single-page application. To deploy on GitHub Pages:

1. Create a new repository
2. Upload `index.html` to the repository root
3. Go to Settings > Pages > Deploy from branch (main)
4. Your site will be live at `https://yourusername.github.io/reponame`

No build step, no dependencies, no server required.
